July, 2022.

Mexicali, Baja California, Mexico.

Photography

By Paul Castillo.

Photoshoot for Expect Pro, a Mexican company that develops audio, video and lighting projects, where it was sought to reflect the meticulous and artisan work behind an audio installation, in addition to highlighting the professionalism and process that the company encompasses.

My inspo.

The main inspirations were photos with low exposure, the grain in photography and the dreamy effect.

About the process.

In this project, 3 consecutive days were worked accompanying the installation crew.

First getting involved in the environment of the audiovisual installation and the place where it was made, to proceed to take photographs documenting all the work.
